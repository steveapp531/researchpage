Leading Innovation in Computational Biology

Vision Lab is a cutting-edge research facility at the forefront of artificial intelligence and computational biology. Our interdisciplinary team combines expertise in machine learning, bioinformatics, and molecular biology to tackle some of the most challenging problems in modern medicine.

Established in 2018, we have published over 50 peer-reviewed papers and developed breakthrough algorithms that are now used by pharmaceutical companies worldwide. Our work spans from drug discovery to personalized medicine, always pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
